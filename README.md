# 🚗 Bright Motors Car Sales Analysis Case Study

### 📌 **Problem Statement**

Bright Motors has recently appointed a new Head of Sales with a mandate to expand the dealership network, improve sales performance, and optimize inventory. However, the company lacks clear, data-driven insight into its historical sales performance.

The challenge is to analyze the Bright Car Sales dataset to uncover key business insights that will support strategic decision-making in sales, marketing, and inventory management.
________________________________________

### 🎯 **Aim of the Project**

The aim of this project  is to analyze historical car sales data and generate actionable insights that will help Bright Motors:


•	Improve sales performance

•	Optimize pricing strategies

•	Identify high-performing vehicles and regions 

•	Support data-driven decision-making 
________________________________________

### 📊 **Objectives (Steps Taken to Achieve the Aim)**


### **1. Planning & Data Architecture**


•	Designed a data flow outline: 

o	Data source (CSV/Excel dataset) 

o	ETL process (cleaning and transformation)

o	Storage (SQL environment such as Databricks/Snowflake)

o	Analysis layer (SQL + visualization tools) 

o	Presentation layer (Power BI / presentation slides) 


### **2. Data Collection & Preparation**


•	Converted dataset into CSV format 

•	Loaded data into SQL environment 

•	Cleaned data by: 

o	Removing duplicates 

o	Handling missing values 

o	Converting price columns into numeric format

________________________________________

### **3. Data Transformation**

•	Created calculated fields: 

o	Total Revenue = Selling Price × Units Sold 

o	Profit Margin = (Selling Price − Cost Price) ÷ Selling Price × 100 


####	Categorized vehicles into: 

o	High Margin

o	Medium Margin 

o	Low Margin 


####	Grouped data by:

o	Make

o Model

o Year 

o	Region (City/Province)

o	Time (Month/Quarter/Year) 

________________________________________

### **4. Data Analysis (SQL)**
   

•	Identified top-performing car makes and models by revenue

•	Analyzed relationship between: 

o	Price 

o	Mileage (Odometer) 

o	Year of manufacture 

•	Evaluated regional sales performance 

•	Explored customer purchasing trends

## 📈  Summary of Results

•	Top-performing car brands and models contribute the highest revenue

•	Vehicle year and mileage strongly influence selling price 

•	Certain regions generate higher sales volumes, indicating strong market demand 

•	Customer preferences show trends in fuel type and vehicle categories 

•	Profitability varies significantly across different vehicle segments
